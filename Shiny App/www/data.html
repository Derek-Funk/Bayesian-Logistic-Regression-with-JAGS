• Source

    • UCI Machine Learning Repository: https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Breast+Cancer+Wisconsin+%28Diagnostic%29
    • Kaggle: https://www.kaggle.com/uciml/breast-cancer-wisconsin-data
    • Python's Scikit-learn

  • 569 cell images

    • Target: 357 benign, 212 malignant
    • 10 main variables:
      • radius
      • texture
      • perimeter
      • area
      • smoothness
      • compactness
      • concavity
      • concave points
      • symmetry
      • fractal dimension
    • For each variable, the mean, standard error, and maximum measurement were taken, making for 30 features total

  • Training vs. Test Sets

    • 80% training: 456 cases
    • 20% testing: 113 cases
    • Each set balanced between benign vs. malignant
